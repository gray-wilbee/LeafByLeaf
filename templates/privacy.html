VoiceJournal Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 17, 2026

VoiceJournal is a private journaling and personal organization tool. This policy explains, in general terms, what information VoiceJournal handles and how it is used. It is provided for transparency and is not legal advice.

Information We Collect

VoiceJournal may collect and store information you provide directly, including:

  • Account information, such as username, password hash, approval status, and authentication tokens.
  • Journal entries, transcripts, notes, tasks, topics, entities, tags, settings, and related metadata.
  • Audio files submitted for transcription, which are processed to create journal text.
  • Usage and operational logs needed to run, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the service.

How We Use Information

VoiceJournal uses your information to:

  • Provide journaling, transcription, search, task management, topic/entity organization, and chat features.
  • Authenticate users and authorize access from integrations such as Custom GPT Actions or MCP clients.
  • Process requests through connected AI and infrastructure services when needed to provide app functionality.
  • Maintain reliability, security, debugging, abuse prevention, and service operations.

AI and Third-Party Processing

Some VoiceJournal features may send relevant content to configured AI or infrastructure providers to perform transcription, summarization, extraction, search, chat, or tool-use workflows. The content sent is limited to what is needed for the requested feature or integration.

Sharing

VoiceJournal does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only as needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, protect the service or users, or fulfill actions you request through connected tools.

Data Retention

VoiceJournal keeps journal entries, tasks, topics, entities, settings, and related records until they are deleted by the user or removed as part of service maintenance. Temporary files and operational logs may be retained for a limited time for processing, debugging, and security.

Security

VoiceJournal uses access controls, authenticated sessions, bearer tokens, API keys, and server-side configuration practices intended to protect user data. No system can guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You can choose what content you add to VoiceJournal and which integrations you authorize. You may revoke connected integration access, regenerate API keys where available, and delete or modify content through the app.

Children

VoiceJournal is not intended for children under 13, and it should not be used to knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date above will be updated when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact the VoiceJournal operator through the channel where you received access to the service.
